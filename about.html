About TextDetectorAI

TextDetectorAI is a free and user-friendly tool that helps you detect whether a piece of writing was created by a human or generated by AI models like ChatGPT. It’s built for journalists, educators, recruiters, and curious minds alike.

Our system uses machine learning models to identify patterns typical of AI-generated text. While it's designed to be fast and helpful, it's important to understand that no AI detection tool is 100% accurate, especially for short or ambiguous inputs.
